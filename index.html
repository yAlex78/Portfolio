Although his real name is Alexander, he prefers to go by Alex, as he thinks that it is much shorter and easier to say/write. Alex hopes to major in computer science in the future, and has spent many years learning programming and computer science. In his free time, Alex enjoys listening to music and taking photos. Usually, the photos he takes are of various scenery that he finds pretty or calming to look at. Click here to see some of the photos that Alex has taken.

Overall, Alex is a very quiet person and prefers to do things by himself in a quiet space. He values the tranquility that comes from being in a silent setting.
